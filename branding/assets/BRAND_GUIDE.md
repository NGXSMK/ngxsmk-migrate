# ngxsmk-migrate Brand Guide

## Vision
The future of Angular modernization. An AI-powered engineering platform that empowers developers to upgrade legacy Angular applications to modern standards (Standalone, Signals, Hydration) with autonomous intelligence.

## Core Messaging
- **Tagline**: "AI Powered Angular Modernization"
- **Positioning**: Industrial-grade, safe, and autonomous migration ecosystem.

## Visual Identity
- **Vibe**: Futuristic, clean, engineering-focused.
- **Primary Colors**: Cyber Blue (#00D1FF), Neon Magenta (#FF00E5).
- **Typography**: Modern, technical sans-serif (e.g., Inter, JetBrains Mono for code).

## Voice & Tone
- **Confident**: We know Angular and AI inside out.
- **Transparent**: Every AI action is explainable and auditable.
- **Empowering**: We help developers focus on innovation, not boilerplate upgrades.
